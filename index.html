What I'm building

Active build

MaidLink

Two-sided marketplace for professional cleaning services · Calgary, CA

The business already exists — maids, clients, and jobs running manually. I'm building the platform to automate and scale it.

The flagship feature is an AI Estimator: a client uploads photos of their space, and the tool estimates cleaning time and generates a full checklist. Solves the #1 anxiety — "how much is this going to cost me?" — before a human ever picks up the phone.

Built on AWS Lambda + Amazon Nova Lite. Currently building a scheduling agent using Amazon Nova — agentic AI, not just generative.
